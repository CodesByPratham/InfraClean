Privacy Protocol

InfraClean collects only the information required to respond to inquiries, prepare quotes, and deliver services. This may include your name, phone number, email address, and site details you share through forms, calls, or messages.

We use your information to communicate with you, schedule assessments, and improve service quality. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Operational media such as site photos or inspection visuals are used for internal analysis and reporting. Any public use is done only with prior approval.

For privacy requests, updates, or deletion of your submitted data, contact us directly at ops@infraclean-systems.com.

When you interact with our website, limited technical information such as browser type, device characteristics, and approximate geolocation may be collected for analytics, reliability, and security monitoring.

We retain inquiry and project records only for as long as required for customer support, legal compliance, billing validation, and service quality audits. Retention periods are reviewed periodically.

Access to customer information is restricted to authorized team members and service providers who need it to perform operational responsibilities, and who are bound by confidentiality obligations.

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect submitted information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

If you believe any information we hold is inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated, you may request correction and we will make commercially reasonable efforts to update records promptly.

By using our contact channels and requesting our services, you acknowledge this privacy protocol and consent to data processing strictly for the purposes described above.

Service Terms

All quotes are based on visible site conditions and project scope shared at the time of assessment. Final pricing may be adjusted if on-site conditions differ materially from initial inputs.

Clients are responsible for providing clear access permissions and required operational approvals for service windows, including any building-level or facility-level coordination.

Work timelines are estimates and may shift due to weather, safety constraints, or force majeure conditions. InfraClean prioritizes operational safety over schedule acceleration.

Any concerns regarding completed work should be reported within 7 days of service delivery so our team can review and respond promptly.

Service execution is subject to safe wind conditions, visibility thresholds, and operational readiness checks performed by our flight and safety team before each deployment window.

Where required, clients must disclose sensitive facade materials, electrical exposure points, restricted flight zones, and known structural limitations prior to scheduling.

Unless explicitly stated in writing, quotation values do not include third-party permit fees, special access machinery, or extraordinary post-clean restoration beyond standard scope.

Payment milestones, invoice timelines, and applicable taxes are governed by the accepted quotation and purchase confirmation issued for each project.

Any service delay resulting from denied access, unsafe conditions, client-side scheduling changes, or incomplete prerequisites may require rescheduling and revised timelines.

InfraClean reserves the right to suspend or terminate operations when safety risks are identified, without liability for delays necessary to protect personnel, property, or surrounding public areas.
